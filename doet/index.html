The Design of Asus Monitor

The monitor I used to cast contents from my laptop has many amazing features, but there are also some features that are not straight forward for the users to understand with a simple glance. There might be a few reasons for this, i.e, the the designers of the product might thik the end users of their products are tech savy, although tech savy people widely use monitors, they are not the only one's who use computers to perfom their day to day tasks. older people, or other groups of the society might use this technology so it should be very user friendly and very descriptive.

Unfortunatley my ASUS monitor is amongst the product that failed to meet usablity because:

  • The product has one Type-c port and three buttons, but only the power button has a discriptive sign is next to it.
  • It doesn't have easy access to adjust screen brightness.
  • If users run into connectivity issue, its hard to find customized troubleshooting tools on the company website.
