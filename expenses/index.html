Usability Problem

Spreadsheet is one of the way people use to manage their financial transactions. Trinity and Neo also uses Spreadsheet to keep track of their credit card purchase on their joint and individual credit cards. while observing Trinity and Neo's spreadsheet I found some interesting usablity problems associated with Learnablity, efficiency and safety.

Lets look Learnablity first. As an external person looking into their spreadsheet It took me a while to understand the structures of it. Although each column is labled, its not direct and clear for an outsider. They also might get confused whey they look back to it after a while. Besides labeling, the fact items are not ordered in alphabetical order makes it disturbing and difficult to find a specific expense. if the expenses were arranged in a certain order it will help to understand it better. The same problem can also be assesed from efficiency and safetey perspectives.

The last but not the least Usability problem observed was safety. if Trinty or Neo have made a mistake on inputing an expense then it's not easy to spot the mistake. From the safety aspect, its crucial to prevent error or spot it quickly before damage happened. If we see the big picture in the aspects big financial companies such a small mistake could lead to a huge financial loss.
