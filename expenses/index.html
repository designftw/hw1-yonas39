Although spreadsheet is a great way of managing financial transactions, In the case of Trinity and Neo Spreadsheet, I observed some usablity problems i.e, learnablity, efficiency and safety. Forexample, if Trinty or Neo have made a mistake on labling an expense correctley then it's not easy to spot the mistake. According to safety aspects of usablity error prevention or immidiate detection are important to reduce the assiciated risk otherwise it could lead to monetary loss. learnablity is also the biggest issue that I have noticed in the Spreadsheet. As seen on the spreadsheet expenses are listed in a random order which makes it difficult to find a certain type of expense, i.e., if the expenses were organized by their alphabetical order or the amount of money it would have been easier to find a certain item quickley. Due to the above mentioned reason we can conclude that the spreadsheet has learniblity and efficiency problems.
